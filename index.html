Our Core Features

Customize Your Profile

With Canary Cloud, you are given the option to stylize your profile to your liking. For no cost, you can; add a profile picture, add a banner picture, write a bio, add your pronouns, and choose a color for your profile!

Find a Community

Canary Cloud is a great place to have a community! Canary Cloud servers allow you to make channels for different topics, make roles with their own unique colors and permissions, and utilize bots!

Make Friends

Canary Cloud is full of friendly people! Canary Cloud moderation ensures bad apples aren't allowed on the platform. If you make a friend, you can use our direct messaging feature to talk to them privately!
